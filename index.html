Secure and Safe Electronic Box

Looking for a free and secure app to store all your private info in one place?

eSecureBox is a free Password Manager with simple and user friendly design. Use eSecureBox to store all your private information in one place and get them anywhere you want. eSecureBox stores all your logins, credit cards, wallet items, accounts, and notes. So, you can keep track of all your personal information in your box anytime, anywhere. We encrypt all your data using high level encryption standard to make them safe and secure. Furthermore it has a strong password generator to make your passwords stronger.

But It's not all the story of eSecureBox, Just start with what you need. Give us feedback and get more features in the future.
